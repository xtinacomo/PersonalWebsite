Christina Como

Bio

I am an enthusiastic computational neurobiologist with 9 years of experience applying molecular, cellular, and bioinformatic approaches to study the developing nervous system. I am fascinated by evolutionary biology and advancing therapeutics for neurological disorders.

I have extensive experience analyzing single cell RNA sequencing (seq), bulk RNA-seq, ATAC-seq, CUT&RUN/ChIP-seq, and spatial transcriptomics using R, shell scripting, and Python.

Outreach

Colorado Research Experiences (CORE) Program Director | 2022 - Present

CORE is a program aimed at creating opportunities for undergraduate students from backgrounds that have been historically underrepresented in science. The 8-week internship will culminates in poster presentations as part of a campus-wide summer research symposium. Participation in the program provides students with hands-on experience in a laboratory environment, training to succeed in scientific careers , and a network of faculty and graduate students to provide longitudinal mentorship. My role as a program director is to develop and execute program plans and growth, manage program budgets, and foster a welcoming and effective program for students. https://www.cu-srtp.com

Advocates for Mental Health (AMH) Program Director 2019- Present

AMH is an organization that strives to promote mental health awareness and support in higher education without stigma. https://advocatescuamc.wixsite.com/amhatcuanschutz
